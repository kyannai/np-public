Key Insights for Facility Manager

  • Tennis Courts drive 77% of all bookings and 53.8% utilization -- the most popular facility by far. Weekend and evening slots are near capacity.
  • 7 of 11 Dining Pavilions are under 15% utilization -- Botanical, Tropical, and Tea pavilions average under 6%. Repricing (reduce or waive booking fee) could stimulate demand.
  • Function Rooms are at 28% utilization -- moderate but with room to grow. Weekend slots are popular; weekday sessions are mostly empty.
  • 28.7% cancellation rate is significant -- nearly 1 in 3 bookings is cancelled. System auto-cancellations (no payment confirmation) account for a large portion.
  • No deposit forfeits recorded -- 3,512 deposit transactions totaling $703,200 collected across 12 months with zero forfeits. Deposit refunds are processed automatically and not individually tracked as transactions.
  • Net booking fees are stable at ~$9.5K/month (excl GST) -- no significant growth or decline trend. Primarily driven by tennis court fees. Hilife service charge (~5.45%) is collected separately from residents and not included in these figures.
